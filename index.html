About

Marketing meets mission

I'm a marketing and brand creative with a passion for sustainability and community building. As Business Development Manager at Sustainability Creatives, I craft content, grow audiences, and connect people who are building a better world.

My work spans video production, weekly newsletters, LinkedIn content strategy, and event management — from intimate career workshops to multi-city Climate Week activations. I believe great storytelling is how movements grow.

I hold a B.A. in Environmental Studies from UC Santa Cruz (Magna cum laude) and continue expanding my toolkit with coursework in data science, sustainability reporting, and business.
